About us

What is Zenspot?

Our organization Zenspot was founded in 2001 after a group of us who practice yoga together went on vacation. We figured out the perfect cocktail of fun and relaxation. Zenspot has grown to be a huge organization full of fun and loving people.
The team has picked each and every location, with having been there and experienced it themselves so we could make each experience unforgettable. Our included locations are all over the world and there's something for everyone!
Each Vacation includes Luxury stays, food & drinks, excursions and yoga classes.

Our Vision

The vision we had and continue to have has always been to see people truly enjoy their surroundings and body, as that makes a peaceful mind. We believe mixing Culture, nature and yoga is the perfect mix to a truly successful vacation where you actually get to relax. It's more than just a vacation, it is memories with wonderful people, amazing scenery and great food.

Our Goal

Our goal here at Zenspot is that you reach the goals you came into this retreat with, along with some helpful tools to take home with you. We want to provide excellence in service and quality. Most importantly, we want you to make new memories and relax.

Yoga Retreat Locations

Egypt

Egypt is located in the northeastern corner of Africa, one of the most beautiful countries that could exist! Our retreat in egypt compares to no other, if you want to immerse yourself in culture and great yoga, Egypt is the pick.

Chile

We call our Chile retreat our 'Hidden Gem'. Chile's culture and food is like no other. It's amazing scenery and environment makes it the best yoga retreat experience! Join us in Chile next!

Dubai

Dubai is incredible. Our retreats there are overflowing with amazing plans and great yoga instructors. Dubai is about refreshening our mind with learning and experience.

Peru

Another one of our hidden gems! You will never see sights and scenery like this again, This retreat is one of our staff favourites! The food and culture are like no other and the peoplel are exceptional. Get ready for yoga with a great view!

Morocoo

Morocoo is nothing you have seen before and we can promise that! One of the most special places to exist. This retreat is filled with art and culture, of course along with loads of yoga! We have some of the most gifted instructors at this retreat. Join us in Morocoo!

Spain

Our retreats in Spain are very special! the Spain retreat is about socilizing and making human connections, of course along with some incredible sights, food and culture.

Hawaii

Hawaii is a very special place to the yoga world. This place makes you connect with your inner self and refresh your mind and soul, just as this organization aims to provide our clients. Hawaii has to be one of the most beautiful places on earth! the mountains and ocean ground you, making this the perfect location for a yoga retreat!

Australia

Our Australia retreat is so special, it is a perfect location to find a vast of wilderness and connect with nature. We believe that mixing yoga and nature gives you the best outcomes. The animals are a plus! Australia is so unique and incredibly beautiful, this retreats includes a visit to animal sanctuaries which we believe only benefits your hunger for knowledge and adventure.
